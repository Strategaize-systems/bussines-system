- `email_tracking_events`, `deal_products`, `auto_winloss_runs`, `proposal_items`, `proposal_payment_milestones` — Single-Parent.

## Transformation (DEC-305)
Pro Tabelle INSERT-`WITH CHECK` + UPDATE-`WITH CHECK` von pure-OR auf:
```
( (fk_a IS NULL OR EXISTS(can_see_owner)) AND (fk_b IS NULL OR EXISTS(...)) AND ...
  AND (fk_a IS NOT NULL OR fk_b IS NOT NULL OR ... [OR created_by = auth.uid()]) )
OR is_admin()
```
- Jeder **gesetzte** FK muss sichtbar sein → schließt die Cross-Tenant-Injection über den ungeprüften FK-Zweig.
- Tabellen **mit** created_by: all-NULL-Row nur bei `created_by=self` (kein created_by-Spoof).
- Tabellen **ohne** created_by: all-NULL-Row nur via `is_admin()` (Orphan-Semantik wie Bestand).
- SELECT/UPDATE-USING/DELETE-USING bleiben unverändert OR (Read nicht bypassbar).
