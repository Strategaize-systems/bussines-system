### 3. Cron-Audit-Log-Check (2 Min)
```bash
ssh root@91.98.20.191 "
DB=\$(docker ps --format '{{.Names}}' | grep '^supabase-db-' | head -1)
docker exec \$DB psql -U postgres -d postgres -c \"
SELECT action, COUNT(*) as cnt, MAX(created_at) as latest
FROM audit_log
WHERE created_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '30 minutes'
  AND action IN ('click_log_cleanup','ai_followup_run','ai_signal_extract_run')
GROUP BY action;
\""
# Erwartet: ai_signal_extract_run mit cnt >=3 (Cron alle 5 Min)
```
